Theme & Scope

Expanding Human Horizon

Cosmos 2026 gathers top minds at the intersection of aerospace engineering and artificial intelligence to lay the foundation for autonomous galactic exploration.

Exploring self-navigating probes, autonomous rovers, and onboard AI systems capable of real-time decision-making without Earth communication lag.

Cosmic Data & Science

Leveraging deep learning architectures to process massive exoplanetary datasets, gravity waves, and sky surveys to discover new stellar anomalies.

Astrobiology & Habitability
